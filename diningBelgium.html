Belgian Dining Selection


Frites Atelier

Frites Atelier was born out of love for authentic flavors, culinary craftsmanship and boundless hospitality with one unique focus: offering a sensational Frites experience that unites people in their love for this simple and delicious comfort food. Prior to the launch of Frites Atelier, Michelin star chef Sergio Herman spent no less than 18 months developing the unique Frites Atelier-Frites. His focus on the continuous development and improvement of every culinary aspect is second to none. This characteristic and complete approach to taste and feeling has created Frites Ateliers that can adorn the lively streets of world cities.


Amadeus

Amadeus restaurant is a sculptors studio converted into a fine dining setting. This is one of the best restaurants in Belgium Brussels which offers the perfect feel and ambience for a romantic dinner or a friendly get together. The walls are adorned with designs inspired from the figures in the old churches which add a different vibe to the entire area. The speciality food served here which is loved by every frequent visitor include beef carpaccio, spare ribs, ricotta, lasagna and spinach tortellini.


Gaufres and Waffles

In the King's Gallery in the heart of Brussels, Gaufres&Waffles opens on Thursday with recipes developed by star chef Yves Mattagne (of Sea Grill**). In this restaurant, the waffle is no longer a dessert or a sweet snack but rather a meal.The Brussels waffle, also famous among tourists, stands out for its creations with unconventional garnishes such as Americain, duck liver, lobster or salmon. The waffle is also served with sweet toppings (chocolate, apples, pineapple or blueberries).


Park

This is a cool restaurant in Brussels Belgian offering a romantic ambience with cosy and intimate interior setting. It is tucked away in a tranquil location in a mansion house. The mouth-watering blends of European fusion served here are best enjoyed with the fine variety of wine stocked in the restaurant. The most recommended dishes of this place include steaks, scallops and french fries. The excellent service and the high quality of food never fail to impress any visitor.
