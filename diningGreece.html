Lithos

As you enter this newly remodeled restaurant, your eyes will immediately be brought to our open fish display with fresh whole fish from the Mediterranean and sustainably farmed as well. In addition, our selection of quality meats and poultry will entice the palette of all culinary preferences. You will be delighted with the simplicity, our respect for the food and allowing the quality of the ingredients to speak for themselves. Our mission is simply to bring friends and family together to enjoy old world flavors presented with a passion for excellence in dining and hospitality.Lithos is one of the authentic Greek restaurants located in the Psyri neighbourhood. Lithos stands out in this area with its charming interiors, not so heavy on the pocket costs and some delicious seafood and lamb shank. It is a tavern turned into a quaint restaurant. This place is loved even by the vegetarians as an assortment of mouth-watering vegetarian dishes are also served here.

Spondi

Spondi restaurant, founded by Apostolos Trastelis in 1996, has been continuously awarded with Michelin Stars since 2002. Spondi embodies timeless values, premium quality, and authentic aesthetics. With a focus on sustainability and local sourcing, Spondi promises a unique and unparalleled culinary adventure. This is a popular restaurant in Athens, Greece which has been given the status of 2 Michelin star restaurant. The ambience and interiors of this place along with the service of highly palatable dishes make for an incredible and unique dining experience. One can witness an ancient and historic feel to the whole place which is beautifully designed with bricks and stones. The whole place is exquisitely lit up offering a bright and positive feel to the surrounding atmosphere.


Varoulko

As our great poet Odysseus Elytis once famously said, if you deconstruct Greece, you will see at the end that what is left is an olive tree, a vineyard and a fishing boat. In the same land, the famous chef Lefteris Lazarou uses these ingredients to create a unique seafood experience in his restaurant, Varoulko.Varoulko restaurant is ideally located in one of the most spectacular places, Mikrolimano Marina right in front of the sea. The diners here can witness excellent sea views and elegant dining atmosphere. The variety of cuisines offered here have been specially designed by the highly talented in-house chef, Lefteris Lazarou. One must definitely try the dishes from Greek cuisines here.
